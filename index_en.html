About Sky Resolve

The airline customer experience, reinvented in Madagascar

Founded in 2025 by a group of visionary young Malagasy entrepreneurs, Sky Resolve is a company specialized in airline customer service.

Although the company is new, its founders have over five years of experience in customer relationship management in the airline sector. Their goal: to transform every interaction between airlines and their passengers into a smooth, personalized, and memorable experience.

From its inception, Sky Resolve has relied on a solid ecosystem of specialists in every key area from process engineering to data analysis, including training, technological tools, and quality supervision.

What sets Sky Resolve apart is its constant need for innovation. The company continuously invests in digital tools, agile methodologies, and custom training programs.

Its ambition goes beyond simple outsourcing: Sky Resolve aims to become a true commercial springboard for airlines.

Based in Antananarivo, Sky Resolve embodies a new generation of Malagasy companies: bold, competent, and internationally oriented.
